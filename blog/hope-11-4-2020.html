Written by Jake on 11/4/2020.

We are all stressed right now. It is time to pray and study God's Word.

2 Timothy 1:7 ESV

"for God gave us a spirit not of fear but of power and love and self-control."

1 Peter 5:7 NIV

"Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you."

Colossians 3:15 ESV

"And let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, to which indeed you were called in one body. And be thankful."

Romans 12:2 ESV

"Do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that by testing you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect."

Commentary

Let us not forget that God is still in control. As long as we serve Him, He will help us get through this.

We must not do what the media claims we do. We must act honorably and properly.
