Written by Jake on 12/4/2020.

There is nothing directly wrong with a biased media source.

The problem with biased media lies in the fact reporting. A biased media source is OK to use as long as they do not let their bias cover up the facts.

Since November, we have seen many media outlets show their true colors with their 2020 election coverage. For example, most major media outlets (CNN, ABC, MSNBC, Twitter, Rueters, etc) are reporting that Joe Biden is the president-elect. This is not accurate. He is just a canidate until the eclors vote for the electoral college and the election is certified. Until then, things can still change and he may not win. By these outlets prematurely reporting Joe Bden as president elect, they cover up the facts and election disputes brought forth by the Republican and Green Parties.
