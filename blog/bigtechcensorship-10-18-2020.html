Written by Jake on 10/18/2020.

Big tech companies have been censoring news articles, politicians, political campaigns, and conservative individuals.

The German Marshall Fund (GMF) has recommended that social networks censor and limit Breitbart, Foxnews, Federalist, and The Blaze. GMF has labeled these organizations as "deceptive." GMF relies on data from NewsGuard, a Microsoft project.

- - -

Twitter and Facebook have been censoring information that does not support Joe Biden, socialism, and COVID-19 lockdowns. These organizations are censoring conservative view points and calling them "misinformation."

- - -

The censorship from big corporations has gotten out of hand. It has become supression of free speech and the press. It is even gone so far as becoming election meddling. By these corporations limiting conservative content, they are impacting the integrity of the 2020 election.
