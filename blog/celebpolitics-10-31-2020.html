OPINION: Celebrities need to not tell people how to vote

  • Home
  • Blog
  • OPINION: Celebrities need to not tell people how to vote

Written by Jake on 10/31/2020.

We Americans do not need the Hollywood alite to tell us how to vote.

Being famous does not make you a political expert.

With this being said, they do have the right to voice their opinions, but demanding or ordering fans to vote for a specific person is wrong. These people lie for a living, much like politicians. They act like they support the everyday people, while they live in $15 million mansions. There is nothing wrong with them having money, but acting like they know how to help people politically does not help.

I watch films to get away from politics, not for politics.
