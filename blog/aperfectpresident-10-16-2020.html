A perfect President?

  • Home
  • Blog
  • A perfect President?

Written by Jake on 10/16/2020.

There is no perfect person. There is no perfect government. There is no perfect president.

But, we can have a good president.

We need to remember when voting for president, we are voting for a national leader, not a spiritual leader.

George Washington was not perfect. Lincoln was not perfect. Trump is not perfect. All of them were/are good presidents. We need to have presidents who support the idea of America. The idea of America is that we the people of the United States of America are created equal by God and we all have equal rights. We are to have the freedom of speech, press, business, and religion. The current democratic platform does not support this idealogy.

The current democratic platform supports crime, murder, control, and China. I believe that Biden (D) would be a bad president.

The current republican platform supports order, life, freedom, and America. I believe that Trump (R) is a good president.
