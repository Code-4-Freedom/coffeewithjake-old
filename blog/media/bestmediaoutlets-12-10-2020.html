What are the best news outlets that you should read?

First thing we have to do, is establish criteria for dertermining what makes a "good" news/media outlet.

Main things to look at:

  • Bias
  • Crediblity
  • Lean

Bias

When reviewing a media outlet, we want to examine and determine if the outlet has a bias (Political view does not mean the source is biased). Dertimining bias requires examining the language and wording of political news articles. If the source speaks poorly of certain people or groups, they are likely biased. You can also determine if their bias is a problem if the outlet is not posting about certain political issues.

Credibility

When reviewing credibility, we want to review whether or not the outlet cites or names their sources for the article. Articles that are from an "anonymous source," are not as credible because you can not verify the information or if the source is an authority on the subject.

Lean

A news outlet having a political lean is not a bad thing, as long as that lean does not prevent proper journalism. We can see this with some of the mainstream news outlets today. They refuse to write anything negative about the democratic party or platform.

Our points scale (The fewer the better):

  • Bias impacts journalism (+1)
  • Is biased towards/against a group (+1)
  • Relies on "anonymous tips" for many articles (+1)
  • Does not cite/name sources
  • Protects a politcal party/view from criticism (+1)
