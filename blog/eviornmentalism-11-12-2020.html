Written by Jake on 11/12/2020.

Modern enviornmentalism is the new communism.

The modern enviornmentalists are using enviornmental problems as a way to take control of our daily lives and liberties.

They want to limit how we can generate electricity. They preach about wind and solar energy, but what they do not mention is the enviornmental impact of wind and solar.

Wind turbines are harmful to the enviornment and the economy.

In order to make a wind turbine, companies have to mine many tons of material and then smelt that material in carbon producing facilities.

Wind turbines are being built in untouched enviornments that were previously undeveloped. This is counter-productive.

Wind turbines also kill between 140,000 and 328,000 birds annually in the USA alone.

Solar farms are harmful and inefficient.

Solar farms are harmful to the enviornment. In order to build a solar farm, companies have to clear large amounts of land and keep those areas clear.

By doing this, these companies are tearing apart the natural enviornment.

For example, let's look at Aerospace Valley (AKA Antelope Valley) in California.

First of all, the area is probably one of the most technologically advanced communities in the United States. The community is largelly responsible for US space travel, super sonic flight, generation five fighters, and more. The community's leaders have decided to use imminant domain and build large solar farms in areas that use to be home to the California Poppy. The leaders did not give the community of engineers and scientists a chance to build a better energy. They decided that solar is best while ignoring the brilliant population.

Solar farms are inefficient.

The max energy generation capabilities of solar, is 33%. However, solar farms on average run at about 27%. These systems take in a huge amount of energy and in return put out hardly anything and damage the natural landscape.

Say no to communism and modern enviornmentalis. Say yes to nuclear.
