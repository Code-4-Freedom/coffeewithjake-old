Written by Jake on 11/11/2020.

Election fraud is real. Here is a brief overview of this week's suspected voting fraud information.

Georgia and a couple other states have been at the center of the 2020 election disputes, investigations, and audits. According to Breitbart News, Georgia has begun a full recount and audit to ensure vote accuracy.

A USPS worked has blown the whistle on fraud and has been met with coersion from federal agents to recant his declaration.

Trump has requested an emergency injuction in Michigan over the state's abnormal voting data and trends.

There has been suspicion of dead votes casted in Georgia and Michigan. The Trump campaign found four obituaries for individuals that "voted" in the election.

The Dominion voting software has had glitches that have caused votes to be improperly counted for Biden. The exact number is unknown, but suspected to be between thousands and millions.
