Written by Jake on 10/7/2020.

I am excited to announce that as of today, the Coffee With Jake blog will be moving away from Blogger (A Google product)!

Google for many years as been silencing conservatives, stealing data, and manipulating information, and I have had enough!

"What was the straw that 'broke the cammel's back?'" you may ask.

Google blocked me from implementing Adsense on my blog.

"Why?"

For violating their guidelines. What do I post? Conservative content and tech reviews. I have NEVER posted inappropriate content on the internet.

This is just another example of big tech censorship.

As part of my response to leaving Blogger, I am developing my own blogging system that works with most hosting services (even GitHub).

I will make the platform available to public once I get all of the bugs worked out.

The older blog posts will still be available at blog.coffeewithjake.com
