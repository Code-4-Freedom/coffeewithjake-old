Written by Jake on 12/3/2020.

US citizens have patient autonomy and self determination.

Because of this right, health departments and other governing agencies should not order the use of masks. Masks are medical devices and the wearing of a mask is a medical decision. Thus, we should have the right to decline wearing masks. Patient autonomy cannot be denied without proper legal action or if the patient is unable to make a decision.

If a person is mentally fit and competent, they make their medical decisions.

Sources:

"Right To Autonomy And Self Determination", USLegal

"Patient Autonomy", ADA
