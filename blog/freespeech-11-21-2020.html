Tried and true free speech resources.

Social/Chat

1: Gab

Gab is an anti-censorship social and media network.

2: Signal

Signal is a privacy/free speech chat platform.

3: Parler

Parler is pro-free speech social platform centered on news and politics.

Browsing

1: Tor

Tor is the best browser for getting around censorship in or to have a voice.

2: Dissenter

Dissenter is the best browser for finding your voice. Dissenter is made by Gab.

3: Beaker

The Beaker Browser is great for those who want to join the decentralized internet. Beaker allows you to host your own website without additional hardware and for free.

Honorable Mention

Keybase

Keybase is a multi-purpose platform that includes privacy chat, free web hosting, secure file sharing, a crypto wallet, groups, and more.
