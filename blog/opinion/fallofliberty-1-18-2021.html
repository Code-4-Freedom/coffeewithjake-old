Written by REDACTED on 1/18/2021.

Liberty has been under attack for years and true liberty has officially fallen.

Big tech corporations have now stolen the last bit of liberty, thought.

Consistent with the Webster 1828 dictionary, "liberty" is "Freedom from restraint, in a general sense, and applicable to the body, or to the will or mind. The body is at liberty when not confined; the will or mind is at liberty when not checked or controlled. A man enjoys liberty when no physical force operates to restrain his actions or volitions."

First, politicians implemented excessive restraint to "protect" human beings from COVID-19.

Now, big tech has stolen free speech and free thought. Big tech's censoring of conservatives is the theft of free speech. Big tech's censoring of right or middle aligned media is the robbery of free thought. Now humans and media groups can only say or write about accredited topics and only in guide of one facet of the political aisle.

Sources:

"Liberty", American Dictionary of the English Language
